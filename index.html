The Rules
Know before you tee
🏌️
Format: 3-player matchups scored hole-by-hole. Low net score wins. Round 4 is a scramble — lowest gross wins.
🥇
Points: 1st = 2 pts, 2nd = 1 pt, 3rd = 0 pts.
🔨
Hammer: Each team can hammer 1 round. Their points double that round (4/2/0). One use per team, whole trip.
⛳
Chulligan: 1 per player per round. Use on any shot.
🔄
Live Sync: Scores update in real time for all players. Hit "Save & Sync" after entering a round to push to everyone.
