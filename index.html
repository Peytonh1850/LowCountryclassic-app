The Rules
Know before you tee
🏌️
Format: 3-player matchups, hole-by-hole. Low net score wins. Round 4 scramble = lowest gross wins.
🥇
Points: 1st = 2 pts, 2nd = 1 pt, 3rd = 0 pts.
🔨
Hammer: Each team hammers 1 round. Their points double (4/2/0). One use per team.
⛳
Chulligan: 1 per player per round, any shot.
🔴
Live sync: Scores push to everyone the moment you hit Save. Leaderboard updates in real time.
